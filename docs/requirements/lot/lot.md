# h24-lot

A lot represents a distinct item or collection of items that are up for sale during an auction event. Each lot is associated with relevant data detailing its auction status and history.


```
Auction
│
├── Lot 1
│   ├── currentBid
│   ├── bids
│   ├── sold
│   ├── product
│   └── ...
│
├── Lot 2
│   ├── currentBid
│   ├── bids
│   ├── sold
│   ├── product
│   └── ...
│
└── ...
```
